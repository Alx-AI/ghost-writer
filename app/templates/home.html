Our Features & Services

STEP 1

Choose the genre you wish for the Ghost Writer to write in.

STEP 2

Either insert a previously written work, or simply press the generate button to receive a tailored excerpt to add to your literary inspiration.

STEP 3

Once you have received your new text, hit generate one more time if you are not satisfied with the current product, or keep it and continue on your writing journey!

Writer's block is a common problem among authors. According to Sarah J. Ahmed and her study regarding it, the cause is often perfectionism or fear of critique which stifles the writing process. Many writers feel pressured to write a perfect first draft and refuse to write out of fear for revising. 

The University of Purdue states that brainstorming and outlining are an efficient solution, but there is a way to take it further. That's why our team developed Ghostwriter. Through AI, authors can generate text by inserting their unfinished writing or ideas. The result creates a basic and easy to manipulate starting point that can be developed, revised, and redone.

How it was Built

The program is built off of multiple Eleuther AI models of GPT-Neo. GPT-Neo is an implementation of model & data-parallel GPT-2 and GPT-3-like models, utilizing Mesh Tensorflow for distributed support. 

Each model was trained on their separate genre. Each model is trained using ~15 books worth of data. After the training is complete, it's able to continue prompts the user puts in and can even generate it's own stories.    

Our Dataset

We started by choosing the specific genres that we wanted to explore. This included fantasy, romance, horror, historical fiction, dystopian, and science fiction. We began collecting our dataset by extracting ~15 novels for each genre, achieving so by converting .pdf files of the specific books to .txt files. 

After all the data was collected, we concatenated all txt files in each genre, ending up with a single txt file for each genre.
